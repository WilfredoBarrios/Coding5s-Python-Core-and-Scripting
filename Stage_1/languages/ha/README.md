## 🚀 Yadda za a fara?

Domin fuskantar wannan dakin gwaje-gwaje a aikace, kuna buƙatar shigar da **Livebook** a kwamfutarku tare da haɗin gwiwar yanayin gudanarwa (runtime) na Python.

1. **Shigar da Livebook:** Sauke shi a [livebook.dev](https://livebook.dev).
2. **Bude dakin gwaji:** Bincika manyan manyan fayiloli (folders) na ƙasa sannan ku danna maballin **"Run in Livebook"** a cikin kowane fayil, ko kuma ku shigar da URL kai tsaye cikin zaman Livebook ɗinku.

---

## 📚 Hanyar Koyarwa ta Coding5s (Matakai 5)

Hanyarmu ta ginu ne akan **Gogayya ta Hankali Mai Sarrafawa** (Controlled Cognitive Friction). Kowane dakin gwaje-gwaje zai jagorance ku ta hanyar:

| Mataki | Suna | Manufa |
|-------|------|-----------|
| **1** | Gwaji (Practice) | Rubuta kodi tun daga farko |
| **2** | Gyara Kuskure (Debug) | Neman kurakuran nahawun kodi (syntax) da na dabara da aka sanya da gangan |
| **3** | Cika Gurbi (Complete) | Cike gibin tsari (architectural) da na algorithm |
| **4** | Gyaran Tsari (Refactor) | Inganta kodin da ke akwai (tsari maras rassa vs. tsari mai rassa/modular) |
| **5** | Fadadawa (Extend) | Ƙara sabbin ayyuka da ke shirye don amfanin masana'antu (production-ready) |

*Wannan ma'ajiyar (repository) ta ƙunshi **Mataki na 1 (Gwaji)**.*

---

## 🛠️ Yadda ake amfani da kowane Livebook

A cikin kowane fayil na `.livemd`, bi wannan tsari mai sauƙi na aiki:

1. Bude fayil ɗin a cikin **Livebook** (sigar tebur ko ta binciken gidan yanar gizo).
2. Kwafi **prompt na Mataki na 1** sannan ku manna shi a cikin tattaunawar AI da kuka fi so (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, da sauransu).
3. AI zai samar da **kananan misalan kodi 3 zuwa 5 masu aiki** don wannan rukunin.
4. Kwafi waɗancan misalan zuwa cikin **wurin gudanarwa** (execution cell) na Livebook sannan ku kunna su.
5. Canza lambar kodi, kuyi gwaji, sannan ku kalli sakamakon yana canzawa a lokacin gaske (real-time).

---

## 🧠 Jagoran Socratic (AI)

Kowane Livebook yana haɗawa da **prompt na Jagora (Mentor prompt)**. Kuna iya manna shi a cikin tattaunawar AI ɗaya tare da kodin ku da takaitaccen bayanin dabararku. Jagoran zai:

- **Ba zai ba ku amsa kai tsaye ba** (a farko)
- Zai yi muku tambayoyi na Socratic domin ku gano kuskuren da kanku
- Zai yi amfani da misalan rayuwa ta gaske don bayyana **ra'ayoyin shirye-shiryen kodi** (programming concepts)
- Zai taimaka muku kawar da "Vibe Coding" (rubuta kodi ta hanyar hasashe) da gina tabbataccen tunani na aikin injiniya
- Zai ba da amsoshi na matakin kwararru (Senior-level) lokacin da kuka yi takamaiman tambayoyi

## 🏛️ Tsari & Jagorori

Kowane dakin gwaje-gwaje ya ƙunshi **Kofar Bayani** (Explanation Gate) inda kwararren AI zai jagorance ku ta hanyar tsarin koyo. Kowane mataki yana da nasa halin jagora na musamman, wanda aka tsara shi don dacewa da bukatun tunani da koyarwa na wannan matakin:

| Mataki | Jagora | Hali | Abin da Yake Koyarwa |
|-------|--------|-------------|------------------|
| **1** | Amintaccen Jagora | Mai girmamawa, mai hakuri, kamar wa ko yaya | Binciken nahawun kodi cikin aminci, kawar da "vibe coding" |
| **2** | Abokin Nazari | Mai son sani, mai kuzari da kofi, "dan sandan log" | Karanta alamun kuskure (error traces), fahimtar dalilin rugujewar tsari |
| **3** | Tsarin Dabara | Mai tsari, mai ƙarfafa gwiwa, "babban magini" | Hango yadda bayanai ke gudana (Input → Transform → Output) |
| **4** | Mai Suka Mai Kyau | Mai ba'a, mai tsauraran ra'ayi, mai amfani da "soyayya mai tsanani" (tough love) | Rubuta ingantaccen kodi (idiomatic code), kawar da munanan halaye |
| **5** | Abokin Tsare-tsare | Mai hangen nesa, kwararre, "injiniyan tsari (system architect)" | Lamuran musamman (edge cases), jure kuskure, daidaita kodi da tsarin kasuwanci |

*Wannan ma'ajiyar ta ƙunshi **Mataki na 1 (Amintaccen Jagora)**.*
