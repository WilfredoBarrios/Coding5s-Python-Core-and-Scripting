## 🚀 Kunjamasa qalltañani?

Aka yatiqañ uta [interactively] (chikanchatawa) yatiqañatakixa, machina k'ikatamanxa **Livebook** ukatxa Python [runtime integration] (pacha lurawi mayacht'ata) uchatapuniwa munasinwa.

1. **Livebook uchamawa:** [livebook.dev](https://livebook.dev) ukanxa apaqamawa.
2. **Yatiqañ uta jist'aramawa:** Aynacha k'atanaka (folders) uñjamawa ukatxa sapa qillqatana **"Run in Livebook"** [button] (ñit'iña chimpu) ñit'imawa, jan ukasti URL ukxa Livebook ukaruwa chiqa apantantmawa.

## 📚 Coding5s Thakhinaka (5 T'aqanaka)

Thakhisaxa **[Controlled Cognitive Friction] (Uñjata lup'iwi ch'amanchawi)** ukaruwa yamp'atawa. Sapa yatiqañ utaxa akhamawa irpiriwa:

| T'aqa | Suti | Amtita |
|-------|------|-----------|
| **1** | Yatiqawi | [code] (qillqata lurawi) machaqata qillqamawa |
| **2** | [Debug] (Pantjasiwi askichaña) | [syntax] (qillqa k'ikata) ukatxa [logic] (lup'iwi lurawi) pantjasiwinaka jikxatamawa |
| **3** | Phuqhachaña | [algorithms] (thakhi lurawi) ukatxa [architectural gaps] (taku lurawi ch'usanaka) phuqhachamawa |
| **4** | [Refactor] (Sumaqtaña) | [scripts] (qillqata lurawinaka) sumaptayamawa (pampa jan ukasti t'aqata taku) |
| **5** | Jach'aptaña | [functionalities] (machaq lurawinaka) yapañamawa |

*Aka imaña utaxa **1 T'aqa (Yatiqawi)** utjiriwa.

## 🛠️ Kunjamasa sapa Livebook apnaqaña

Sapa `.livemd` qillqata manqhanxa, aka pampa lurawi thakhiwa arkamawa:

1. **Livebook** ukanxa (machina k'ikata jan ukasti maskiri) qillqata jist'aramawa.
2. **1 T'aqa [prompt] (lurawi mayiwi)** kopyamawa ukatxa AI (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude, ukatxa juk'ampi) aruskipawimanxa k'askatamawa.
3. Uka AI ukaxa uka amtatakixa **3 ukata 5 cama [micro-examples] (jisk'a luriri uñacht'awinaka)** luraniwa.
4. Uka uñacht'awinakxa Livebook uka **[execution cell] (luriri t'aqa)** ukaruwa kopyamawa ukatxa t'ijuyamawa.
5. [code] (qillqata lurawi) mayjt'ayamawa, yant'amawa, ukatxa [output] (misturi) kunjamasa mayjt'i ukxa uñjamawa.

## 🧠 Socrático Yatichiri (AI)

Sapa Livebook ukaxa **[Mentor prompt] (Yatichiri mayiwi)** utjarakiwa. Uka pachpa AI aruskipawiruwa [code] (qillqata lurawi) ukatxa jisk'a [logic] (lup'iwi lurawi) arxatawimampi k'askatañamawa. Yatichirixa:

- **Janiwa chiqa t'aqxa churkätamti** (qalltanxa)
- Socrático jiskt'anaka jiskt'atamwa, ukhamata jumapachpa pantjasiwi jikxatañamataki
- **[programming concepts] (qillqawi lup'iwi amuyunaka)** qhananchañatakixa aka pacha uñtasitanaka apnaqaniwa
- "Vibe Coding" chhaqhayañatakixa yanapt'atamwa ukatxa chiqa [engineering intuition] (lurañ amuyu) luraniwa
- Suma jiskt'anaka jiskt'atamanxa, [senior-level answers] (yatiri jach'a t'aqanaka) churaniwa

⚠️ JACH'A LURAWI YATIYAWI MAYA JAN UKASTI NATIVE ARUNAKATAKI: Suma aru jark'aqawi, [inline comments] (manqha qillqa amuyunaka), ukatxa [dynamic glossaries] (sarnaqiri aru pirwanaka) maya arunakatakixa (Maya arunaka ukhamaraki) suma uñjañatakixa, Gemini Pro (jan ukasti juk'ampi) apnaqañawa wali askiwa. Ch'usa-qullqi AI ukaxa janiwa aka arunakatakixa suma [syntactic attention] (qillqawi uñjawi) utjkiti, kunatixa AI yatichawi qillqanaka jisk'akiwa. Abya Yala Maya ukatxa native arunakatakixa, suma [outputs] (misturinaka) jikxatañatakixa [context seed] (thakhi jatha) [prompts] (mayiwinaka) manqharu uchatawa; ukatwa, suma misturi jikxatañatakixa jila-qullqi AI mayitawa.

## 🏛️ [Architecture] (Taku lurawi) & Yatichirinaka

Sapa yatiqañ utaxa **[Explanation Gate] (Qhananchawi Punku)** utjiwa, ukanxa maya yatiri AI Yatichiriwa yatiqawi thakhina irpatamwa. Sapa t'aqaxa yatichiri ajayu utjiwa, uka pacha munawinakatakixa (psychological and pedagogical) luratawa:

| T'aqa | Yatichiri | Ajayu | Kuna Yatichisa |
|-------|--------|-------------|------------------|
| **1** | Jark'aqata Irpiri | Junt'u, suyiri, jila kullaka uñtasita | Suma [syntax] (qillqa k'ikata) thaqhawi, "vibe coding" chhaqhayawi |
| **2** | Tupuqi Masimasi | Jiskt'iri, cafe-umata "[log detective] (pantjasiwi thaqhiri)" | [error traces] (pantjasiwi thakhinaka) ullarawi, [crashes] (t'unjawinaka) amuyawi |
| **3** | [Logical Scaffold] (Lup'iwi Ch'amanchawi) | Thakhiniri, ch'amanchiri "[senior builder] (jach'a luriri)" | [data flow] (k'ikata thakhi) uñjawi (Input → Transform → Output) |
| **4** | Suma Uñjiri | Sarcástico, q'uma, "q'ayma munasiwi" | Suma [code] (qillqata lurawi), [functional patterns] (lurawi saminaka), jan walt'ata lurawinaka chhaqhayawi |
| **5** | Thakhiri Masimasi | Uñjiri, [professional] (yatiri) "[system architect] (lurawi taku luriri)" | [Edge cases] (K'acha thakhinaka), [fault tolerance] (pantjasiwi aguantawi), [business logic] (qullqi lurawi lup'iwi) |

*Aka imaña utaxa **1 T'aqa (Jark'aqata Irpiri)** utjiriwa.
